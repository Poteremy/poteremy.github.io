Surprise!

We're having a baby!

And we are just as surprised as you are. We know there are going to be questions. To avoid having to answer the same questions many times, we put together this nifty little FAQ that will hopefully address a lot of those questions.

FAQ

How did this happen?

Aside from the actual answer of how it happened, we're not really sure to be honest. We were actively trying for a while, and then when Tara's health concerns became what they are, we took a break from actively trying. And then one day, POOF! A magical pregnancy test came back positive. She took a few tests over the course of the next few days just to be sure, and that was that!

When is the baby due?

Our last ultrasound has the baby's due date on November 10th, 2022.

Does Tara's POTS pose any risk to the baby?

Not inherently. Along with POTS, Tara is diagnosed with Vasovagal Syncope. The only real risk posed to the baby would be if she lost consciousness which resulted in direct trauma to her belly. The condition itself does not pose any risk.

Do Tara's seizures pose any risk to the baby?

No. Just like the POTS and Vasovagal Syncope, the seizures themselves do not pose any risk by themselves. The only risk associated with the seizures would be if there was any direct trauma to her belly.

How is Tara feeling?

It varies day to day. Emotionally? Dealing. Physically? Varies even more significantly day to day. The good news is her cardiologist said she might actually see her symptoms improve in the second trimester. So we're hopeful.

Are you going to find out the gender?

We will probably find out the gender through a blood test when we can. However, we do not plan on announcing the gender and instead want it to be a surprise for everyone when the baby is born.

Is Ryder excited to be a big brother?

Yes, Ryder is very excited to be a big brother. He is constantly asking to see the baby, asking if it's kicking, and refers to it as 'his baby friend'.

Is there anything you need?

We managed to get a lot of things at a yard sale recently. However, as we prepare the house and start moving things around into a nursey, we'll undoubtedly come across things we need. We just can't give a list just yet. However, cash donations and gift cards are always appreciated and will go a long way :]

Do you have a baby registry?

Not an official one yet, but when we do, we'll send it out and post it here.
